Who we are
Get to know a little bit about Company 84

It all started in pops garage about two years ago. On a search for new furniture for my apartment, I couldn't find things that were quite right. Some items were just way out of the price range I was looking at and others (and even many of the expensive ones) were just very poorly made. I figured I'd give it a shot.

My ol' man has a massive garage with about every tool one can imagine. I went at it. Cutting too short, ripping boards wrong, but I practiced. I got better. I really enjoyed it. After making a few tables and lamps, I decided to try and sell a few. And I did. A coffee table here, a lamp there, and things started to take off.

Now here we are! In the process of finding a location to build permanently and continue to bring awesome furniture items to awesome people! We encourage you to take a look around and connect with us using your favorite method below.

Our Process
This is how we roll

Your new piece of furniture all starts as an idea. We'll discuss, sketch and get on the same page for your idea. Materials and finishes will be determined by bringing you as many samples as needed. We'll make sure the wood and metal is prefect so we assure you a quality item. We build it, we deliver it. You will be sure to get the proper care instruction upon delivery. We look forward to create the perfect piece of furniture for any room.
